Solo Nightmare

With this simulator you are able to simulate x kills of solo Nightmare. This simulator literally rolls on a loot table with the approximated rates from the wiki. This simulator isn't going to necessarily give you the average, or likely, results from x kills. Because it is rolling just as it would in-game it is possible, but unlikely, to get rare abnormal results; just like in-game.

Max 20,000, min 1

The COMMON drops may be slightly inaccurate, at the time of writing this all the wiki has is the drop ranges for common drops but not the distribution rates, so this simulation assumes that the drop ranges have a constant distribution, ie the max possible amount of a drop is just as likely as the least amount of a drop.
