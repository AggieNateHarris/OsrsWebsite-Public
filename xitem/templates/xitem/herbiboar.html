About

With this simulator you are able to simulate x kills of herbiboar. This simulator literally rolls on a loot table with the approximated rates from the wiki, assuming 99 herblore and magic secateurs are used. This simulator isn't going to necessarily give you the average, or likely, results from x kills. Because it is rolling just as it would in-game it is possible, but unlikely, to get rare abnormal results; just like in-game.
