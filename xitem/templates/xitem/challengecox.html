About

With this simulator you are able to simulate obtaining certain items at solo challenge cox. This simulator literally rolls on a loot table with the approximated rates from the wiki, assuming 60k points per raid. This simulator isn't going to necessarily give you the average, or likely, results from x kills. Because it is rolling just as it would in-game it is possible, but unlikely, to get rare abnormal results; just like in-game.
