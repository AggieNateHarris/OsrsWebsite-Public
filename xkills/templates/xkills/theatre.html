About

With this simulator you are able to simulate x completions of Theatre of Blood. This simulator literally rolls on a loot table with the approximated rates from the wiki, assuming 0 deaths and team of 3. Rares shown consist of all rares for the entire team, whereas the common drops are for one individual. This simulator isn't going to necessarily give you the average, or likely, results from x kills. Because it is rolling just as it would in-game it is possible, but unlikely, to get rare abnormal results; just like in-game.

Max 10,000, min 1
