About

With this simulator you are able to simulate x kills of the Giant Mole. This simulator literally rolls on a loot table with the approximated rates from the wiki. This simulator isn't going to necessarily give you the average, or likely, results from x kills. Because it is rolling just as it would in-game it is possible, but unlikely, to get rare abnormal results; just like in-game.

The seed table is a simulation of taking all the hides and claws received from your x kills and trading them in at Wyson for nests and then opening those nests.

Max 20,000, min 1
