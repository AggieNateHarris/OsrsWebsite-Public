About

With this simulator you are able to simulate opening x hard clue caskets. This simulator literally rolls on a loot table released by j-mods which can be found here: drop table. This simulator isn't going to necessarily give you the average, or likely, results from x caskets. Because it is rolling just as it would in-game it is possible, but unlikely, to get rare abnormal results; just like in-game.

Max 20,000, min 1
