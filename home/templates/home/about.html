SimulationScape Goal

The goal of SimulationScape is to give players helpful tools and insight into Old School Runescape, the game that we all love. The initial tools that I'm intending to create are tools that help satisfy curiosity; ever wanted to know the total loot you would get from 10,000 zulrah kills? Cox? A simulation that rolls on their loot tables as many times as you want is one such tool. Not only will it satisfy curiosity but it can be helpful in estimating certain loot that you will get from various bosses.

About Me

Hi! My name is Nate and I've been playing runescape for over 10 years. I began back in middle school when my friends found the game on miniclip and we all spent countless hours exploring and enjoying the MMO experience that was completely new to us. Fast forward some years and old school was released, I had grown tired of the original runescape and jumped onto osrs the first day it launched.

I spent my years in the original rs as a pker but began making the switch to a skiller around the time of osrs being released. Initially in osrs my goal was to make a 'piety pure' and I was the first person to have the 60 att 70 def piety build in old school runescape. I always enjoyed runecrafting and it was how I made my money and I began doing other skills, I was one of the first people in osrs to get 91 rc for the best money at the time, double nature runes. I consistently held around rank 100-200 area in the overall highscores for the first 3 years or so of osrs while working a job. With my best overall ranks being around rank 60. I was around the 130th person to max total level in osrs. I held the record play time for achieving barrows gloves for a period, taking just 28 hours 33 minutes from a brand new account to having completed Recipe For Disaster.

Since the original runescape I've been making videos and really focusing on it more at the beginning of osrs as I began to really compete for ranks and firsts, unlike I ever had in the original runescape. My videos focused mostly on my progress through the game, guides, pking, and game updates.

I went to university for computer science and that's where my focus is currently. I don't have as much time to play anymore but I still enjoy spreading the knowledge of the game that I gained over the years and thousands upon thousands of hours of playing the game.
